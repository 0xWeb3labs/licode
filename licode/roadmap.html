1. Current Projects

Licode offers video, audio and data communication to several projects today!

  • MCS5:

  • FI-CONTENT:

    Licode is being implemented in a SmartCity application to show tourist videos to several users. 2. Future Enhancements

Licode is growing very fast, with new enhancements and features, such as Firefox compatibility and RTSP support.

We are currently working to develop the next features in the short term:

  • Recording of video and audio in rooms.
  • Support to other browsers: Ericsson Bowser.
  • Include external streams from RTMP, raw RTP, and so on
  • Improve monitorization and notifications about rooms, users and streams
